Sugaring NYC is happy to introduce you to V-FACIALS ADD-ONS that you can get RIGHT AFTER your bikini sugaring, when your skin is hairless, exfoliated and pores are open. This makes the add-ons more effective, since all the products can penetrate deeper into the skin for better results.

 *Can be done on bikini, underarms, inner thighs and butt cheeks.

What is a V-Facial ? For those of you new to the concept, the V-facial is a facial for bikini area. 

Benefits:
1) Treats ingrown hairs, bumps and assists with prevention.
2) Reduces redness & hyperpigmentation.
3) Improves skin texture, promotes skin renewal & cell turnover.
4) Removes impurities, controls sebum & detoxes the skin.
5) Deep hydration for lasting softness, smoothness & moisturized skin.

Ingrown Hair Extractions -$20

We will steam the affected area and soften up the pustules for easy ingrown hair removal.  We will remove the hairs that are ready to be extracted. This is for anyone who has a breakout of in grown hairs , especially those that have painful pustules.

Chemical Peel - $20

Our Glycolic Acid chemical peel is a super exfoliation that acts like a pac man and eats away at that which clogs the pores causing the hair to get trapped underneath. This is ideal for someone who gets deep and pitted ingrown hairs as well as those who want to more even skin tone and deeper results to keep the area clear of future ingrown hairs.   Those who are allergic to aspirin or that are currently taking any retinol are not a candidate for this service.

 

Mask (anti-ingrown, moisturizing, whitening, calming, vitaminazing, and more) - $20

We offer a range of masks to address your skins specific concern. Your  Sugarista will evaluate your skin and discuss with you the mask that is being selected and why.  Our masks range from ones that help treat hyperpigmentation to ones that are pore refining to ones that are meant more for hydration. When you come in for your service you will speak with your Sugarista to select the best one for your skin.

High Frequency Machine - $10

High frequency is a popular skincare technique that is used to treat a variety of skin conditions such as ingrown hairs. By pushing oxygen deep into the pores  it eliminates bacteria. It is highly recommended  that if you do extractions that you also do high frequency. Anyone is a candidate unless you have a pacemaker or are pregnant.

* When all 4 services are performed, we call it "All Inclusive V-Facial", but you also have an option to simply choose one of these add-ons or "Pick Any 2", "Pick Any 3" and save money.
